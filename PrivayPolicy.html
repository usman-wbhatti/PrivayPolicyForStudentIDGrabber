Privacy Policy

The Student ID Grabber extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

All data processing (extracting Student IDs and email addresses from the currently open page) happens locally on your computer. No information is ever sent to external servers.

The only time data leaves the page is when you explicitly copy it to your clipboard by clicking the “Copy” button in the extension popup.

We do not collect analytics, we do not use cookies, and we do not share data with any third parties.
